News Image & Special Reports

For 70 years, WGAL has been the local television leader in the Susquehanna Valley. In the early days of television, WGAL’s strong signal helped the station establish dominance as the viewers’ preferred choice.. for both local news and national programming. Even today, with so many options available to TV homes, WGAL remains the strongest method for reaching the most viewers in the Susquehanna Valley. When you buy advertising with WGAL, you’re not limited to just one powerful station. The combination of our main affiliation with NBC, America’s #1 TV network, and our secondary/subchannel affiliation with MeTV, a leader in providing high-quality “memorable entertainment” favorites, allows WGAL to reach more audience segments across the area at every time of day. WGAL remains the ratings leader in local news… We offer more local news from 4PM to 6:30 than any other station. This dominance is part of our history, but history would mean nothing if we didn’t continue to prove our dedication to the community… with quality journalism, presented by experienced professionals. As part of the prestigious Hearst family of television stations, WGAL maintains a dedication to fact-checked, impartial journalism… from the local level to the national stage. We are bound by a commitment to our viewers… to take them where news is happening, bringing them the facts that matter… on every screen. By offering you our streaming news options along with your on-air buy… you can make sure you’re reaching the most viewers possible.
